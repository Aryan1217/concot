Overview

This is an event that provides participants a platform to showcase the brightest minds from various schools. Our aim is to create an unparalleled platform for collaboration, innovation, and skill development by designing an Instagram page.

Eligibility Criteria

For classes 9th-12th

Participation

The team shall consist of 3 members: videographer, video editor & graphic designer. The team should complete all the tasks under the time limit of 2.5 hours.

Rules and Regulations

  • The team has to prepare an Instagram page consisting of 5 posts (3 graphic posts and 2 reels).
  • The theme/topic of the page will be given on the spot.
  • The judgment will be based on the creative outlook.
  • Participants should bring their own equipment (lights, laptop, camera, pendrive, etc.).
  • INTERNET WILL BE PROVIDED
  • The team cannot use templates to create reels.

Prizes

1st position: Cash prize of Rs. 3000
2nd position: Cash prize of Rs. 2000
3rd position: Cash prize of Rs. 1000
